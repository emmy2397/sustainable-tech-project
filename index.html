You're about to make an investment and buy a product. But can't make your mind up whether you should really buy it or not.

You take part in our questionnaire that was designed to help you make the most sustainable decision - for you and the environment.

You will receive one of three possible outcomes after participating: buy product immediatly, rent it, reconsider before buying the product, or don't buy it at all.

You'll be able to share a personalized picture of what you did not consume in case you chose the last option that you can share with your friends and family and challenge them in their future buying and consumption behavior.
